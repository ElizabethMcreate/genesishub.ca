About Us

A trusted hub for community support and health-focused empowerment.

We work alongside individuals, families, volunteers, and partners to respond to real community needs with empathy, accountability, and purpose. Our approach is rooted in dignity, inclusion, and practical support that helps people move from crisis toward stability and growth.

Through collaborative programs, outreach initiatives, and referral pathways, Genesis Community & Health Support Centre creates meaningful opportunities for healthier lives, stronger families, and more connected communities.

Our Programs

Responsive services designed around community needs.

Our programming is built to meet people where they are, reduce barriers to support, and create pathways toward long-term wellness and resilience.

🩺

Community Wellness Support

Holistic support services designed to strengthen individual and family wellbeing through outreach, guidance, and referrals.

🍽

Food & Basic Needs Assistance

Responsive support for individuals and families facing hardship through food hampers, essential supplies, and coordinated assistance.

🎓

Youth & Family Empowerment

Programs that build resilience, life skills, and stronger connections for youth, parents, and caregivers.

🤝

Settlement & Community Navigation

Practical help to connect community members with services, information, and trusted pathways to thrive.
